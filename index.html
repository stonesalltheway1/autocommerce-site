How AutoCommerce Works

Our autonomous system streamlines the entire e-commerce arbitrage process from finding opportunities to making sales.

1

Market Research

AutoCommerce continuously scans Amazon, eBay, and Walmart to collect product data, pricing information, and market trends using both API integrations and advanced web scraping technologies.

2

Arbitrage Analysis

Our system analyzes price differences between platforms, calculates potential profit margins, shipping costs, and identifies the most lucrative arbitrage opportunities that meet your minimum profit thresholds.

3

ML-Powered Verification

Machine learning algorithms validate arbitrage opportunities by analyzing product similarity, seller ratings, historical price data, and market demand to ensure maximum profit potential.

4

Automated Execution

With optional auto-execution features, the system can automatically purchase products from the source marketplace and list them on the target platform with optimized pricing and descriptions.

5

Order Management

AutoCommerce tracks orders across all platforms, manages shipping details, updates tracking information, and ensures smooth fulfillment to maintain high seller ratings.

6

Profit Analysis

Comprehensive reporting tools track all transactions, calculate actual profits, analyze ROI, and provide actionable insights to continually improve your arbitrage strategy.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoCommerce.

What is e-commerce arbitrage?

E-commerce arbitrage is the practice of buying products from one marketplace at a lower price and selling them on another platform for a profit. AutoCommerce automates this entire process, from finding opportunities to pricing optimization.

Do I need API access to marketplaces?

No, AutoCommerce works with both API integrations and advanced web scraping technology. While API access can enable additional features, the system can operate effectively using just web scraping for marketplace data collection.

Can I customize profit thresholds?

Yes, AutoCommerce allows you to set custom minimum profit amounts and ROI percentages. You can define your own criteria for what constitutes a worthwhile arbitrage opportunity based on your business goals.

What technical requirements are needed?

AutoCommerce is built in Python and can run on Windows, macOS, or Linux. The system requires Python 3.8+ and approximately 4GB of RAM. All dependencies are automatically installed during the setup process.

Is the software cloud-based or local?

AutoCommerce can be deployed both locally on your computer or on a cloud server. The web dashboard can be accessed from any device with a web browser, allowing you to monitor your operations from anywhere.

How accurate is the AI for price optimization?

Our machine learning models achieve an average of 85-90% accuracy in price prediction across diverse product categories. The system continues to learn and improve over time as it collects more data about your specific market segments.
